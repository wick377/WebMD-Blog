Use Google Classroom for Distance Learning

doctorHimself

Classroom makes it easy for learners and instructors to connect—inside and outside of schools.
The Classroom is integrated with other services like Google Docs for writing, Gmail for communicating, Google Calendar for scheduling and Hangouts Meet for video calling.

Set Up Your Google Classroom

Google Classroom is accessible via your webmd.tech account. You will simply need to go to classroom.google.com. From here, you will follow the steps for creating your classroom. You will be able to add various classes and sections as needed.
After the class is set up, then it is time to sign your students up. They will need to sign in with the class code, which Google will generate. At this point, your Classroom is ready for use.

Set Up Virtual Meeting Using Hangouts Meet

Meet is integrated into Google Classroom to quickly generate managed video calls.
Educators can create a unique Meet link for each class, which is displayed on the Classroom Stream and Classwork pages. The link acts as a dedicated meeting space for each class, making it easy for both teachers and students to join.
Learn more about using Hangouts Meet in Google Classroom

Use Classroom Calendar

For each class, you share a Classroom calendar and a Google Calendar with your teacher and classmates.
You can see due dates, class events and scheduled Meet sessions in the calendar.
Learn more about using Calendar with Google Classroom
